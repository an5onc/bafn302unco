Course Learning Objectives

  • Understand and apply the time value of money in financial decision-making
  • Analyze financial statements and interpret key financial ratios
  • Evaluate the risk-return relationship and apply the CAPM model
  • Use capital budgeting techniques (NPV, IRR) to evaluate projects
  • Calculate and interpret cost of capital and WACC for investment decisions
  • Apply working capital management principles to optimize cash flow
  • Value stocks and bonds using appropriate valuation models
  • Synthesize concepts to make integrated financial management decisions
