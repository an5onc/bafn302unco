Understanding Retirement Planning

4% Rule

A common guideline suggesting you can withdraw 4% of your portfolio annually in retirement without running out of money over 30 years.

Compound Interest

Your earnings generate their own earnings. Starting early gives compound interest more time to work its magic.

Dollar Cost Averaging

Regular monthly contributions help smooth out market volatility by buying at different price points over time.

Inflation Impact

Money loses purchasing power over time. A 2.5% inflation rate means prices double approximately every 29 years.
