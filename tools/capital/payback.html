Payback Period

💡
Why This Matters

Payback period measures how long it takes to recover your initial investment from project cash flows. Simple payback ignores time value of money; discounted payback accounts for it using your discount rate. Shorter payback = faster capital recovery = lower risk.

Key Concepts:
  • Simple Payback = Years to recover I₀ from undiscounted cash flows
  • Discounted Payback = Years to recover I₀ from PV of cash flows
  • Decision Rule: Accept if Payback ≤ Required Payback Period
  • Limitation: Ignores cash flows after payback; biased against long-term projects
