Expected Return

💡
Why This Matters

Expected return is the probability-weighted average of all possible returns. It tells you what you expect to earn on average, accounting for uncertainty in each outcome.

Formula:
  • r̄ = Σ (pᵢ × rᵢ)
  • pᵢ — probability of scenario i (must sum to 1)
  • rᵢ — return in scenario i (enter as % e.g. 12 for 12%)
