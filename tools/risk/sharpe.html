Sharpe Ratio

💡
Why This Matters

The Sharpe Ratio measures return above the risk-free rate per unit of risk. It answers: "Am I being compensated for the risk I'm taking?" Higher Sharpe = better risk-adjusted performance.

Formula:
  • Sharpe = (rₚ − rf) / σ
  • rₚ — portfolio or asset return (%)
  • rf — risk-free rate (%)
  • σ — standard deviation of returns (%)
