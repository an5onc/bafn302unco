Coefficient of Variation (CV)

💡
Why This Matters

CV = σ / r̄ measures risk per unit of return. It lets you compare two assets with different expected returns on a level playing field — lower CV = more efficient risk.

Formula:
  • CV = σ / r̄
  • Enter σ and r̄ directly, or compute from probability scenarios below.
