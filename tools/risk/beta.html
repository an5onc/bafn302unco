Why This Matters

Beta measures a stock's sensitivity to market movements. β = 1 means the stock moves with the market. β > 1 is more volatile than the market. β < 1 is less volatile.

Formulas:
  • Direct mode: Enter β directly
  • Cov/Var mode: β = Cov(Rᵢ, Rₘ) / Var(Rₘ)
