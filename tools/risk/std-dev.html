Standard Deviation

💡
Why This Matters

Standard deviation measures the spread of possible returns — it is the primary measure of risk in finance. A higher σ means more variability (more risk).

Formulas:
  • Prob mode: σ² = Σ pᵢ(rᵢ − r̄)²; σ = √σ²
  • Historical (sample): σ = √[Σ(rᵢ−r̄)²/(n−1)]
  • Historical (population): σ = √[Σ(rᵢ−r̄)²/n]
