Portfolio Beta

💡
Why This Matters

Portfolio beta is the weighted average of individual betas. It tells you how sensitive your overall portfolio is to market movements.

Formula:
  • βₚ = Σ (wᵢ × βᵢ)
  • βₚ = 1 → moves with market | βₚ > 1 → more volatile | βₚ < 1 → more defensive
