Two-Asset Portfolio Risk

💡
Why This Matters

Portfolio risk is NOT simply the weighted average of individual risks — correlation (ρ) determines how much diversification benefit you actually get. Lower ρ = more diversification = lower portfolio risk.

Formula:
  • σₚ = √(w₁²σ₁² + w₂²σ₂² + 2w₁w₂σ₁σ₂ρ)
  • ρ = −1: perfect negative correlation (maximum diversification)
  • ρ = 0: uncorrelated assets
  • ρ = +1: perfect positive correlation (no diversification benefit)
