P/E Stock Valuation

💡
Why This Matters

The P/E method estimates intrinsic value by applying an industry or sector average P/E ratio to the firm's earnings per share. Simple and widely used — especially when dividend data is unavailable.

Formulas:
  • P₀ = EPS × P/E — intrinsic value
  • Implied P/E = Market Price / EPS
  • EPS = Net Income / Shares Outstanding
  • Compare intrinsic P₀ to market price to assess over/undervaluation
