Dividend Yield & Capital Gains Yield

💡
Why This Matters

Total required return breaks into two parts: the income you receive today (dividend yield) and the growth in price you expect over time (capital gains yield). In the Gordon Growth Model, capital gains yield equals g.

Formulas:
  • r = Dividend Yield + Capital Gains Yield
  • Dividend Yield = D₁ / P₀
  • Capital Gains Yield = g (for constant growth)
  • r = D₁/P₀ + g
