QQQ Timeline

Report-style timeline showing how this holding fits into the broader five-year project story.

Jan 5, 2021

Project Buy Date

32.07 shares purchased at $311.86. QQQ enters as the Nasdaq-100 growth ETF — heavily concentrated in mega-cap tech.

Nov 19, 2021

QQQ all-time high at $408

Peak of the post-COVID growth-stock rally on near-zero rates and pandemic-era tech demand. Rate-hike fears begin pulling growth lower into year-end.

2022

Worst growth-stock year since 2008

Long-duration growth gets crushed by 425 bp of Fed hikes — DCF math punishes future-cash-flow stocks hardest. QQQ falls 33% peak-to-trough, bottoming Oct 13, 2022.

Nov 30, 2022

ChatGPT launches — AI trade ignites

Generative AI reframes the tech investment thesis. Nvidia, Microsoft, Meta, and Alphabet — all top QQQ holdings — become the "Magnificent Seven" leadership trade through 2024.

2024–2025

AI capex super-cycle

Hyperscaler AI infrastructure spending crosses $200B/year. QQQ's tech-heavy weighting captures the super-cycle better than SPY does, opening a performance gap.

Apr 27, 2026

Final tracking date — $664.23

QQQ closes at $664.23, position worth $21,301.86 — best performer in the project (+113.02% total, 15.31% CAGR). The reward for accepting growth-concentration risk.

May 24, 2023

Nvidia's "shot heard round the world" earnings

Nvidia guides Q2 revenue $4B above consensus on AI-chip demand. Stock gaps +24%, single-handedly lifting QQQ +1.7% the next day. The AI trade goes mainstream.

Jul 24, 2024

"Magnificent 7" rotation scare

Tesla and Alphabet earnings disappoint; QQQ drops 3.6% — its worst day since 2022. Highlights the concentration risk: top 7 holdings drove most of QQQ's gains.

Jan 27, 2025

DeepSeek shock

Chinese AI model launched at fraction of U.S. training cost. Nvidia drops -17% in a day (largest single-day market-cap loss in history) — QQQ closes -3%, recovers within weeks.

Reflection Draft

QQQ helps explain why investors accept more volatility for the chance of a higher return. It finished ahead of every other position, but that result came from heavier growth exposure.
