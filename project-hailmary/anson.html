Portfolio Timeline

How the five positions evolved through the tracking period.

Jan 5, 2021

All five positions purchased

$50,000 deployed across Tesla, SPY, QQQ, VUG, and AGG at January 5, 2021 closing prices.

2021

Growth and EV momentum

Post-pandemic enthusiasm lifted growth stocks and EV names like Tesla. QQQ, VUG, and TSLA all benefited from strong market momentum.

2022

Rising rates change everything

The Fed's aggressive rate-hike cycle hit long-duration growth assets hard. Both TSLA and the bond fund AGG faced significant pressure during this period.

2023–2025

Recovery and compounding

QQQ and VUG recovered strongly as large-cap growth resumed leadership. SPY climbed steadily. Tesla was volatile but ultimately recovered from its 2022 lows.

Apr 8, 2026

Portfolio reaches $78,310.35

The combined five-position portfolio is up $28,310.35 from the starting value of $50,000, even with AGG still below cost.
