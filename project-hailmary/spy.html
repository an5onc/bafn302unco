SPY Timeline

Report-style timeline showing how this holding fits into the broader five-year project story.

Jan 5, 2021

Project Buy Date

26.93 shares purchased at $371.33. SPY enters as the broad-market benchmark — the yardstick every other position is measured against.

Jan 3, 2022

SPY all-time high at $479

The S&P 500 closed at a record high on the first trading day of 2022, capping the post-COVID rally before the Fed pivoted hawkish.

Mar 16, 2022

Fed begins fastest hiking cycle in 40 years

First 25 bp hike kicks off a 425 bp rate-hike cycle to fight 9.1% CPI inflation. SPY enters a bear market and bottoms at $348 on Oct 12, 2022 — down 27% from the peak.

Q4 2023

Fed pivot fuels recovery

Powell signals rate cuts coming in 2024; SPY rips 16% in two months as soft-landing thesis takes hold. The "Magnificent Seven" mega-caps lead the rebound.

2024–2025

AI capex super-cycle

Hyperscaler AI spending and the start of Fed rate cuts (Sept 2024) push SPY past $580. Mega-cap concentration grows — top 10 holdings hit ~37% of the index.

Apr 27, 2026

Final tracking date — $715.17

SPY closes at $715.17, giving the position a value of $19,259.53 — a +92.60% total return and 13.15% CAGR. Solidly outperformed Tesla with no single-company risk.

Jun 10, 2022

CPI hits 9.1% — 41-year high

Hotter-than-expected inflation print triggers a 75 bp Fed hike — the largest single move since 1994. SPY drops 5.8% the following week.

Mar 10, 2023

Silicon Valley Bank fails

Second-largest bank failure in U.S. history. SPY drops 4.5% in a week on regional-bank contagion fears before the Fed's emergency BTFP backstop calms markets.

Sept 18, 2024

First Fed rate cut since 2020

50 bp cut — larger than expected — confirms easing cycle. SPY closes at a new all-time high; broad market rally extends through year-end.

Reflection Draft

SPY makes the strongest case for diversification. It was not as dramatic as Tesla, but it produced a larger ending value with broad market exposure and less dependence on one company.
