Portfolio Timeline

A combined timeline showing how the five positions help tell the full market story.

Jan 5, 2021

All five investments are purchased

The project starts with five separate $10,000 positions, for a combined simulated portfolio value of $50,000.

2021

Growth and equity momentum

Broad equity and growth exposure were supported by strong post-pandemic market momentum. Tesla, QQQ, and VUG all fit naturally into that narrative.

2022

Rising rates change the environment

Higher rates pressured both long-duration growth assets and bonds. That helps explain why AGG lagged and why growth holdings became more volatile.

2023–2025

Diversification starts to matter

Instead of depending only on one stock, the portfolio benefited from broad market exposure through SPY plus targeted growth exposure from QQQ and VUG.

Mar 27, 2026

Checkpoint value reaches $75,050.78

By the progress report, the combined five-position portfolio was up $25,050.78, even with AGG still below cost.
